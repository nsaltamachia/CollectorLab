About This Collector

You know how people like to joke: "If I won the lottery, I wouldn't tell anyone. (but there'd be signs)?"


That'd be me for sure.

I've been a die-hard lover of automobiles ever since I was using my brother's Matchbox cars as pacifiers.

I love everything about cars, ALL cars from ALL locations and times. Their development has been closely-linked with the development of the world that drives them since their own beginning, when Henry Ford (yeah, THAT Ford) invented the assembly line...
